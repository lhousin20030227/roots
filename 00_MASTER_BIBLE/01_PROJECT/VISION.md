## Vision Statement

ROOTS is a story-driven first-person mystery game in which the player uncovers the truth through observation, exploration, and evidence rather than combat or supernatural events. The goal is to create an emotional and believable experience that feels personal, immersive, and memorable.

---

# Design Goals

* Deliver a strong emotional story.
* Reward curiosity and exploration.
* Let players discover the truth by themselves.
* Avoid unnecessary gameplay systems.
* Keep every mechanic connected to the narrative.

---

# Player Experience

The player should constantly feel curious, emotionally connected, immersed, thoughtful, and rewarded after each discovery. The game should encourage players to ask questions before giving answers.

---

# Storytelling Philosophy

The story is discovered, not explained. Information is revealed through documents, photos, audio recordings, objects, locations, dialogue, and environmental details. Long cinematic cutscenes should be avoided whenever possible.

---

# Gameplay Philosophy

Gameplay must always support the narrative. Every objective should naturally lead the player toward discovering more evidence and unlocking new locations.

The core gameplay loop is:

Explore
→ Observe
→ Collect evidence
→ Solve puzzle
→ Discover new truth
→ Unlock new area

---

# Development Vision

The project must remain achievable by a solo developer using free tools and AI assistance. Complexity should come from story design, world building, and investigation mechanics rather than expensive technology.

---

# Success Criteria

The project is successful if players finish the game feeling emotionally invested, remember its story, and immediately want to continue Part 2. Quality, consistency, and immersion are always more important than adding more features.
