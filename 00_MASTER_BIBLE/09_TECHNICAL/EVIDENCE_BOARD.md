# EVIDENCE BOARD

## Purpose

The Evidence Board is the player's central investigation tool.

It automatically organizes collected evidence and helps the player connect clues without solving the mystery for them.

---

# Philosophy

The Evidence Board is an organizational tool.

It does **not** reveal hidden answers.

The player is responsible for making logical conclusions.

---

# Board Sections

## Documents

Stores all written evidence.

Examples:

* Birth Certificate
* Adoption Papers
* Medical Records
* Letters
* Diaries
* Official Reports

---

## Photographs

Stores all collected photographs.

Players can:

* Zoom
* Rotate
* Inspect details
* Compare multiple photos

---

## Audio Recordings

Contains every discovered recording.

Examples:

* Voice Messages
* Cassette Tapes
* Voicemail
* Radio Broadcasts

Players may replay recordings at any time.

---

## Objects

Important physical evidence.

Examples:

* Keys
* Wedding Ring
* Military Medal
* Pocket Watch
* Family Album

Each object includes inspection notes.

---

## Character Profiles

Automatically updated as the player learns more.

Each profile contains:

* Name
* Relationship
* Known Facts
* Related Evidence
* Timeline

Unknown information remains hidden.

---

## Location Profiles

Every visited location has its own page.

Includes:

* Description
* Discovered Evidence
* Connected Characters
* Remaining Points of Interest

---

# Evidence Connections

Related evidence is linked together automatically.

Example:

```text
Birth Certificate
      │
      ▼
Hospital Record
      │
      ▼
Adoption Papers
      │
      ▼
Biological Father
```

The system highlights relationships but never explains their meaning.

---

# Search System

Players can search evidence by:

* Name
* Date
* Character
* Location
* Category
* Keyword

---

# Filters

Available filters include:

* New
* Unread
* Critical
* Optional
* Story Evidence
* Character Evidence
* Location Evidence

---

# Timeline View

The board includes a chronological timeline.

As new evidence is collected, important events are added automatically.

Missing events remain hidden until discovered.

---

# Player Notes

Players may add personal notes.

Notes are completely optional and never affect gameplay.

---

# Design Rules

* Automatically updated.
* Easy to navigate.
* Never spoils future events.
* Never deletes collected evidence.
* Clearly separates confirmed facts from unknown information.

---

# Performance Rules

* Updates instantly after collecting evidence.
* Saves automatically.
* Loads quickly.
* Supports unlimited evidence entries.

---

# Final Rule

The Evidence Board is the player's investigative workspace.

It should help organize information while preserving the satisfaction of discovering the truth through observation, deduction, and careful thinking.
