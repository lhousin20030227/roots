# LOCATIONS

## Purpose

This document defines every major location in ROOTS and its narrative purpose.

Every location must contribute to gameplay, storytelling, and exploration.

---

# 1. Adoptive Parents' House

## Purpose

Starting location and emotional center of the game.

## Gameplay

* Tutorial
* Exploration
* First puzzles
* First evidence

## Key Areas

* Entrance
* Living Room
* Kitchen
* Bathroom
* Player Bedroom
* Parents' Bedroom
* Storage Room
* Attic
* Basement (optional)

---

# 2. City Hall

## Gameplay

* Unlock new investigation paths.

# 6. Cemetery

Closure.

---

# Location Design Rules

Every location must have:

* A believable purpose.
* Unique atmosphere.
* Environmental storytelling.
* Collectible evidence.
* At least one meaningful objective.
* Logical puzzles.
* Realistic layout.

---

# Revisit Rules

Locations may change over time.

Returning later may reveal:

* New dialogue.
* New evidence.
* Newly accessible rooms.
* Story progression.

---

# Final Rule

No location should exist only for decoration.

Every location must answer at least one question while creating another, keeping the investigation moving forward.
