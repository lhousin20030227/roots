# FACTIONS

## Purpose

This document defines all organizations, institutions, and groups that exist in the world of ROOTS.

These are not enemy factions. They are realistic organizations that support the investigation and the story.

---

# Government

Responsible for:

* Civil records
* Identity documents
* Adoption records
* Public administration

The player interacts with government offices to obtain official information.

---

# Healthcare System

Responsible for:

* Birth records
* Death certificates
* Medical history
* Hospital archives

Medical staff provide information only when it is legally and logically available.

---

# Military

The older brother joined the military many years before the events of the game.

The military itself is never the focus of Part 1.

Its role is limited to historical records and future story development.

---

# Local Community

Neighbors, shop owners and ordinary citizens.

They may:

* Remember past events.
* Provide optional dialogue.
* Help connect story details.

Every NPC has a believable life outside the player.

---

# Cemetery Administration

Responsible for:

* Burial records
* Grave locations
* Funeral information

Provides factual information only.

---

# Adoptive Family

The family that raised the player.

Their actions were motivated by love and protection.

They are central to the emotional story.

---

# Biological Family

The player's original family.

Their history forms the main mystery of Part 1.

Every major story event originates from this family.

---

# Design Rules

* No evil organizations.
* No secret societies.
* No criminal gangs as the main focus.
* No supernatural cults.
* Every institution must behave realistically.
* Every organization must have a believable purpose.

---

# Final Rule

Organizations exist to make the world feel authentic, not to create artificial conflict. The story is driven by people and their choices, not by factions fighting each other.
