# BIOLOGICAL FATHER

## Purpose

Defines the biological father as one of the central characters in ROOTS and the emotional core of the second half of Part 1.

---

# Basic Information

**Full Name:** TBD

**Role:** Biological Father

**Status:** Alive during the investigation, dies near the end of Part 1.

**Age:** 55–65 (TBD)

---

# Personality

* Quiet
* Exhausted
* Regretful
* Humble
* Honest
* Caring beneath his suffering
* Emotionally broken

He speaks little, but every word carries weight.

---

# Background

He lived a normal life with his wife and two sons.

After his wife died during childbirth, his life collapsed.

Depression slowly turned into alcohol addiction.

He eventually lost custody of his younger son through legal adoption.

Years later, his older son left to join the military.

From that point onward, he lived almost completely alone.

---

# Motivation

His greatest regret is losing his family.

He never stopped thinking about his children.

Even after many years, he quietly hoped to see them again.

---

# Relationship with the Player

The player begins as a complete stranger.

Trust develops slowly through conversation and shared moments.

Their relationship is built on understanding rather than instant forgiveness.

---

# Health

Years of alcoholism severely damaged his liver.

His illness is irreversible.

By the time the player finds him, very little time remains.

His approaching death gives emotional urgency to the story.

---

# Gameplay Role

The player visits him multiple times.

During these visits the player can:

* Talk with him.
* Learn family history.
* Discover forgotten memories.
* Find personal belongings.
* Collect important evidence.

Each visit reveals more about the family's past.

---

# Narrative Function

He is not the villain.

He is a tragic man whose life was destroyed by grief, loss, and poor decisions.

His story explores regret, responsibility, forgiveness, and the possibility of reconciliation.

---

# Final Scene

Before his death, he reveals one final truth:

The player has an older brother.

This revelation becomes the foundation of Part 2.

Shortly afterward, he passes away peacefully.

---

# Design Rules

* Never portray him as evil.
* Never excuse his mistakes.
* Never reduce him to "the alcoholic father."
* Show both his failures and his humanity.
* His redemption comes through honesty, not miracles.

---

# Final Rule

The biological father represents the cost of unresolved grief.

His final wish is not to erase the past, but to give his child the truth that was lost for many years.
