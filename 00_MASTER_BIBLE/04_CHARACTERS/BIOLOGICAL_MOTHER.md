# BIOLOGICAL MOTHER

## Purpose

Defines the biological mother as one of the most important unseen characters in ROOTS. Although she never appears alive, her life and death shape every major event in the story.

---

# Basic Information

**Full Name:** TBD

**Role:** Biological Mother

**Status:** Deceased before the beginning of the game.

**Age at Death:** TBD (approximately 25–35)

---

# Personality

* Loving
* Gentle
* Responsible
* Optimistic
* Compassionate
* Patient
* Family-oriented

She believed that family always came first.

---

# Background

She married the biological father and together they had their first son.

Several years later she became pregnant again.

During childbirth, complications occurred.

Despite medical efforts, she passed away shortly after giving birth to the player.

Her death became the turning point that changed every member of the family forever.

---

# Relationship with the Family

### Biological Father

They shared a loving and supportive relationship.

Her death left him emotionally devastated.

---

### Older Brother

She was a caring mother.

The older brother remembers her only through childhood memories.

---

### Player

The player never had the chance to know her.

Everything the player learns comes from:

* Documents
* Hospital records
* Photographs
* Letters
* Stories told by others

---

# Gameplay Role

The player gradually reconstructs her life through investigation.

Important evidence includes:

* Birth records
* Medical documents
* Family photographs
* Personal letters
* Personal belongings

Every discovery helps humanize her rather than idealize her.

---

# Narrative Function

She is the emotional starting point of the entire story.

Her death is not the mystery.

Its consequences are.

Every major event in ROOTS can be traced back to that single day.

---

# Design Rules

* Never portray her as a perfect person.
* Never use flashbacks where she directly speaks to the player.
* Let the player know her through evidence and memories.
* Her presence should always feel warm, even in her absence.

---

# Final Rule

The biological mother represents love that was lost too early.

Although she appears only through memories and evidence, her influence is present in every chapter of ROOTS.
