# PLAYER

## Purpose

Defines the playable character and the principles that govern the player's role throughout ROOTS.

---

# Basic Information

**Name:** Chosen by the player at the beginning of the game.

**Role:** Main Protagonist

**Age:** Young Adult (TBD)

**Status:** Alive

---

# Background

The player grew up believing they were the biological child of their adoptive parents.

They had a peaceful childhood and never questioned their identity.

After both adoptive parents pass away, the player returns to the family home to organize their belongings.

During this process, small inconsistencies begin to appear, leading to a life-changing investigation.

---

# Personality

The protagonist is intentionally written as a quiet and observant person.

Core traits:

* Curious
* Empathetic
* Intelligent
* Patient
* Determined
* Emotionally resilient

The player shapes the character's attitude through choices, but never changes the main personality.

---

# Motivation

At first:

* Organize the house.
* Honor the adoptive parents.

Later:

* Understand the strange discoveries.

Finally:

* Discover the truth about their identity.
* Reunite the broken family.

---

# Knowledge

The player begins the game knowing only what a normal person would know about their own life.

Knowledge grows only through:

* Exploration
* Evidence
* Dialogue
* Documents
* Observation

Nothing is magically revealed.

---

# Skills

The protagonist has no superhuman abilities.

They rely on:

* Observation
* Logic
* Memory
* Investigation
* Deduction

---

# Strengths

* Patient thinker.
* Good listener.
* Emotionally mature.
* Curious explorer.

---

# Weaknesses

* Can become emotionally overwhelmed.
* Has incomplete knowledge of the past.
* Must earn the trust of others.
* Cannot solve problems through force.

---

# Gameplay Role

The player performs actions such as:

* Explore locations.
* Collect evidence.
* Read documents.
* Solve puzzles.
* Speak with NPCs.
* Connect clues.
* Unlock new locations.

---

# Character Growth

The protagonist changes through understanding, not through gaining power.

Growth comes from:

* Learning the truth.
* Accepting the past.
* Forgiving others.
* Finding purpose.

---

# Design Rules

* Never give the player supernatural abilities.
* Never make the player a combat hero.
* Never allow choices that contradict the core personality.
* The player should feel like an ordinary person facing extraordinary emotional circumstances.

---

# Final Rule

The protagonist is not trying to save the world.

They are trying to understand who they are, where they came from, and what remains of their family.
