# CHARACTER TIMELINE

## Purpose

This document tracks the life events of every major character to ensure consistency throughout the project.

---

# Biological Mother

### Childhood

* Born.
* Grew up in the town.

### Adult Life

* Married the biological father.
* Gave birth to the older brother.
* Became pregnant with the player.

### Final Event

* Died during childbirth.

---

# Biological Father

### Early Adult Life

* Married.
* Started a family.
* Stable job.
* Happy life.

### Turning Point

* Wife died.
* Developed depression.
* Became addicted to alcohol.

### Later Years

* Lost custody of the player.
* Older son left home.
* Lived alone.
* Health declined.

### Final Event

* Reunited with the player.
* Revealed the existence of the older brother.
* Passed away.

---

# Adoptive Father

### Adult Life

* Married the adoptive mother.
* Could not have children.
* Legally adopted the player.

### Following Years

* Raised the player with love.
* Kept the adoption secret.

### Final Event

* Passed away before revealing the truth.

---

# Adoptive Mother

### Adult Life

* Married the adoptive father.
* Adopted the player.

### Following Years

* Raised the player as her own child.
* Preserved family memories through letters and photographs.

### Final Event

* Passed away before telling the player about the adoption.

---

# Player

### Birth

* Born to the biological parents.

### Childhood

* Legally adopted.
* Raised by the adoptive family.
* Grew up unaware of the adoption.

### Present Day

* Returns home after the funeral.
* Begins investigating.
* Discovers the truth.
* Meets the biological father.
* Learns about the older brother.

---

# Older Brother

### Childhood

* Born several years before the player.
* Lived with both biological parents.

### Youth

* Lost his mother.
* Remained with his father.
* Witnessed the family's decline.

### Early Adult Life

* Left home.
* Joined the military.
* Lost contact with the family.

### Current Status

* Unknown.

His disappearance becomes the primary objective for Part 2.

---

# Timeline Rule

Every future dialogue, document, flashback, and story event must remain consistent with this timeline. Any change requires updating the MASTER BIBLE before implementation.
