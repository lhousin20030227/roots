# ADOPTIVE FATHER

## Purpose

Defines the adoptive father as a character and his role in the story.

---

# Basic Information

**Full Name:** TBD

**Role:** Adoptive Father

**Status:** Deceased before the beginning of the game.

**Age at Death:** 60–70 (TBD)

---

# Personality

* Calm
* Patient
* Responsible
* Honest
* Hardworking
* Protective
* Emotionally reserved

He rarely expressed his feelings through words.

Instead, he showed love through his actions.

---

# Background

Unable to have children, he and his wife legally adopted the player.

From the first day, he considered the player his own child.

He never treated the player differently.

---

# Motivation

His greatest goal was to give the player a safe and happy life.

He believed hiding the truth about the adoption would protect the player until the right time.

Unfortunately, he passed away before revealing it.

---

# Relationship with the Player

* Loving father.
* Teacher.
* Protector.
* Role model.

The player remembers him with warmth and gratitude.

---

# Gameplay Role

Although deceased, his presence is felt throughout the game through:

* Letters
* Notes
* Photographs
* Personal belongings
* The family home
* Stories told by others

He indirectly guides the player's investigation.

---

# Narrative Function

He represents unconditional love.

His decisions create the central mystery of the game, but they were always made with good intentions.

---

# Design Rules

* Never portray him as a villain.
* Never suggest he adopted the player for selfish reasons.
* His flaws should make him human, not evil.
* Every memory involving him should reinforce the emotional foundation of the story.

---

# Final Rule

The adoptive father is not the source of the tragedy.

He is the person who gave the player a home, a family, and a childhood filled with love.
