# OLDER BROTHER

## Purpose

Defines the Older Brother as one of the most important unseen characters in ROOTS and the primary objective for Part 2.

---

# Basic Information

**Full Name:** TBD

**Role:** Biological Older Brother

**Status:** Alive (Current Whereabouts Unknown)

**Age:** Approximately 5–8 years older than the player (TBD)

---

# Personality

* Protective
* Responsible
* Independent
* Quiet
* Resilient
* Loyal
* Emotionally guarded

He learned to hide his emotions after years of hardship.

---

# Background

He was the first child of the biological parents.

He experienced a happy childhood before tragedy struck.

After his mother died, he watched his father's life slowly collapse.

Unlike the player, he remembers everything.

---

# Turning Point

He witnessed:

* His mother's death.
* His father's depression.
* The player's adoption.
* The family's separation.

These events forced him to grow up much faster than other children.

---

# Military Service

As a young adult, he joined the military.

The decision gave him purpose and a chance to leave his painful past behind.

Communication with his family gradually stopped.

Years later, nobody knows where he is.

---

# Relationship with the Player

The player has no memory of him.

The older brother clearly remembers the player as a baby before the adoption.

Finding each other becomes the emotional goal of Part 2.

---

# Gameplay Role

He never appears directly during most of Part 1.

His existence is slowly revealed through:

* Family photographs
* Official records
* Letters
* Conversations
* The biological father's memories

Only near the end does the player learn that he exists.

---

# Narrative Function

He represents the missing piece of the family.

His disappearance transforms the ending of Part 1 into the beginning of a new journey.

---

# Design Rules

* Never portray him as a villain.
* Never reveal his current location during Part 1.
* Keep his fate uncertain.
* Build curiosity through small clues instead of direct answers.

---

# Final Rule

The Older Brother is the bridge between Part 1 and Part 2.

The player finishes ROOTS with one final objective:

**Find the older brother.**
