# RELATIONSHIPS

## Purpose

This document defines the relationships between the main characters in ROOTS. These relationships are the emotional foundation of the story and must remain consistent throughout the project.

---

# Player ↔ Adoptive Father

## Relationship

Father and child.

Although not biologically related, their bond is genuine and built on years of love, trust, and care.

## Emotional State

* Deep respect.
* Gratitude.
* Love.
* Loss after his death.

---

# Player ↔ Adoptive Mother

## Relationship

Mother and child.

She was the emotional heart of the family.

## Emotional State

* Comfort.
* Affection.
* Safety.
* Grief after her passing.

---

# Player ↔ Biological Father

## Beginning

Complete strangers.

The player knows nothing about him.

## Development

* Curiosity.
* Doubt.
* Understanding.
* Compassion.
* Reconciliation.

Trust develops gradually through multiple meetings.

---

# Player ↔ Biological Mother

The player never met her.

Their relationship is built through:

* Letters
* Photographs
* Medical records
* Family stories

The player slowly forms an emotional connection with someone they never knew.

---

# Player ↔ Older Brother

At the beginning of Part 1:

* The player does not know he exists.

By the ending:

* The player learns they have an older brother.
* Finding him becomes the next life goal.

---

# Biological Father ↔ Biological Mother

They deeply loved each other.

Her death devastated him and became the turning point of his life.

Their relationship should always be remembered with warmth and respect.

---

# Biological Father ↔ Older Brother

A difficult relationship shaped by grief and poverty.

Despite their conflicts, love always remained beneath the surface.

---

# Adoptive Father ↔ Adoptive Mother

A stable and loving marriage.

They made every important decision together, including the adoption.

They always acted in what they believed was the player's best interest.

---

# Adoptive Family ↔ Biological Family

There is no hatred between the two families.

They are connected by tragedy rather than conflict.

The story is about loss, love, and difficult choices—not villains.

---

# Relationship Rules

* Relationships evolve naturally.
* Trust is earned over time.
* Forgiveness is never immediate.
* Love is shown through actions more than words.
* Every interaction should strengthen emotional realism.

---

# Final Rule

Every relationship in ROOTS must feel authentic, emotionally grounded, and believable. The player's journey is driven by human connections, making relationships the true heart of the game.
