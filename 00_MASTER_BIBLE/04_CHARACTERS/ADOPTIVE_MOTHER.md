# ADOPTIVE MOTHER

## Purpose

Defines the adoptive mother as a character and her role in the story.

---

# Basic Information

**Full Name:** TBD

**Role:** Adoptive Mother

**Status:** Deceased before the beginning of the game.

**Age at Death:** 58–68 (TBD)

---

# Personality

* Kind
* Caring
* Gentle
* Patient
* Compassionate
* Organized
* Emotionally expressive

She openly showed affection and always tried to make the player feel loved and safe.

---

# Background

Together with her husband, she legally adopted the player after a difficult period in the biological family's life.

She never considered the player "adopted."

To her, the player was simply her child.

---

# Motivation

Her greatest wish was for the player to grow up happy, healthy, and never feel abandoned.

She agreed to keep the adoption secret because she believed the truth should only be revealed when the player was emotionally ready.

She never had the chance to have that conversation.

---

# Relationship with the Player

* Loving mother.
* Emotional support.
* Caregiver.
* Source of comfort.

Most of the player's happiest childhood memories involve her.

---

# Gameplay Role

Although she is no longer alive, her presence remains through:

* Family photographs
* Personal letters
* Recipe books
* Handwritten notes
* Household items
* Memories shared by neighbors

Many discoveries inside the house are connected to her.

---

# Narrative Function

She represents unconditional care, warmth, and family.

Her absence is felt throughout the game and reinforces the emotional weight of the investigation.

---

# Design Rules

* Never portray her as manipulative or cruel.
* Never imply she loved the player less because of the adoption.
* Her kindness should feel genuine and believable.
* Her personal belongings should quietly reveal her personality and daily life.

---

# Final Rule

The adoptive mother is one of the emotional foundations of ROOTS.

Even after her death, the love she gave continues to shape the player's journey and the meaning of the story.
