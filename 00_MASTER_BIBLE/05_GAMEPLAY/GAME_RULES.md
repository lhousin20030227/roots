# GAME RULES

## Purpose

These rules define the fundamental gameplay principles of ROOTS.

Every gameplay system, mechanic, interaction, puzzle, and objective must follow these rules.

---

# Core Principle

Gameplay exists to support the story.

The player progresses by discovering the truth—not by defeating enemies or collecting rewards.

---

# Player Progression

Progress is earned through:

* Exploration
* Observation
* Investigation
* Evidence Collection
* Puzzle Solving
* Dialogue

Progress is **never** based on:

* Experience Points (XP)
* Character Levels
* Skill Trees
* Grinding

---

# Interaction Rules

Every interaction must have a purpose.

Examples:

* Read a document.
* Open a drawer.
* Inspect a photograph.
* Listen to a recording.
* Unlock a door.

Avoid meaningless interactions.

---

# Exploration Rules

Exploration must always reward curiosity.

Rewards include:

* New evidence.
* Story progression.
* Hidden rooms.
* Optional lore.
* Character development.

The player should never explore without purpose.

---

# Puzzle Rules

Every puzzle must:

* Be logical.
* Have a believable context.
* Support the story.
* Be solvable through observation.

Avoid puzzles that exist only to slow the player.

---

# Inventory Rules

Inventory contains only meaningful items.

Every item must have at least one clear purpose.

No junk items.

No random collectibles.

---

# Evidence Rules

Evidence is the primary progression system.

Critical evidence:

* Cannot be permanently missed.
* Must have a logical location.
* Must unlock meaningful progression.

---

# Dialogue Rules

Dialogue should:

* Feel natural.
* Respect the player's progress.
* Change according to discovered evidence.
* Never reveal future information prematurely.

---

# Save Rules

The player may save at any time unless restricted by a scripted sequence.

Saving must preserve:

* Objectives
* Inventory
* Evidence
* Puzzle states
* Dialogue progress
* World state

---

# Difficulty Rules

Challenge should come from:

* Observation
* Logic
* Investigation

Not from:

* Fast reflexes
* Time limits
* Artificial difficulty
* Repetition

---

# Forbidden Gameplay

The following systems are permanently excluded:

* Combat
* Weapons
* Enemy AI
* Boss Fights
* Character Levels
* XP
* Crafting
* Survival Systems
* Hunger
* Thirst
* Stamina
* Magic
* Supernatural Powers
* Multiplayer
* Microtransactions
* Loot Boxes
* Battle Pass

---

# Consistency Rule

Every new gameplay feature must answer **YES** to all of the following:

1. Does it improve exploration?
2. Does it support investigation?
3. Does it strengthen the narrative?
4. Does it increase immersion?
5. Does it justify its development cost?

If the answer is **No** to any of these, the feature should be rejected or redesigned.

---

# Final Rule

ROOTS is an investigation game, not an action game.

Every second of gameplay should bring the player closer to understanding the truth, ensuring that story, gameplay, and emotion always work together.
