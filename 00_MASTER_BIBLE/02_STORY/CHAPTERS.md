# CHAPTERS

## Purpose

The story is divided into chapters. Each chapter has a clear objective, introduces new evidence, unlocks new locations, and moves the player closer to the truth.

---

# Chapter 1 — Homecoming

### Goal

Return to the adoptive parents' house.

### Main Objective

Organize the house after the funeral.

### Player Learns

* The house layout.
* Basic controls.
* Interaction system.
* First hints that something is unusual.

### Unlocks

* Bedroom
* Living Room
* Kitchen
* Storage Room

---

# Chapter 2 — The First Clues

### Goal

Investigate strange personal belongings.

### Main Objective

Find documents that do not match the player's memories.

### Player Learns

* Small inconsistencies exist.
* The parents hid something.

### Unlocks

* Attic
* Locked Cabinet
* Old Photo Box

---

# Chapter 3 — Hidden Truth

### Goal

Discover the adoption.

### Main Objective

Collect enough evidence to confirm the truth.

### Player Learns

* Adoption records.
* Birth certificate.
* Hospital information.

### Unlocks

* Hospital
* City Archive

---

# Chapter 4 — Searching for the Father

### Goal

Find the biological father.

### Main Objective

Follow official records and witnesses.

### Player Learns

* The father's difficult life.
* His addiction.
* His illness.

### Unlocks

* Father's House
* Cemetery
* Medical Records

---

# Chapter 5 — Final Goodbye

### Goal

Help the biological father.

### Main Objective

Spend time with him before his death.

### Player Learns

* Family history.
* The existence of an older brother.

### Ending

The father passes away.

---

# Chapter 6 — A New Beginning

### Goal

Return home.

### Main Objective

Review everything discovered.

The player finds one final photograph showing:

* Biological Father
* Biological Mother
* Older Brother

The player leaves the house carrying the photo.

Fade to black.

**End of Part 1.**

---

# Chapter Design Rules

Every chapter must contain:

* A clear objective.
* New evidence.
* At least one meaningful puzzle.
* Character development.
* Story progression.
* A rewarding conclusion.
