# LORE

## Purpose

The world of ROOTS is completely grounded in reality.

There are no supernatural events, paranormal entities, magic, or unexplained phenomena.

Everything that happens has a logical explanation.

---

# World

The game takes place in a quiet modern town.

People live ordinary lives.

Schools, hospitals, archives, cemeteries and government offices operate normally.

The town itself is not the mystery.

The mystery belongs to one family.

---

# Family History

Many years before the events of the game:

A young couple had two sons.

Life became increasingly difficult.

Financial problems grew.

The mother died while giving birth to the younger child.

The father fell into depression.

Eventually he developed alcohol addiction.

Unable to care for both children, the family was separated.

The younger child was legally adopted.

The older child left years later to join the military.

The family disappeared from public life.

---

# Adoption

The adoption was completely legal.

The adoptive parents genuinely loved the player.

They never intended to hurt or deceive them.

They believed revealing the truth later would be less painful.

Unfortunately, they both passed away before telling the truth.

---

# The Biological Father

The father was not an evil man.

He was a broken man.

His greatest regret was losing his children.

Years of addiction destroyed his health.

By the time the player finds him, he is already terminally ill.

---

# The Older Brother

Very little is known.

Only scattered records remain.

His disappearance becomes the central mystery for Part 2.

---

# Philosophy

ROOTS tells the story of ordinary people facing extraordinary emotional situations.

The world is realistic.

The emotions are real.

The mystery exists because people hide painful truths—not because of supernatural forces.
