# TIMELINE

## Purpose

This timeline defines the chronological order of all major events in the ROOTS universe. Every story element, document, dialogue, and gameplay event must remain consistent with this timeline.

---

# Years Before the Game

## ~30 Years Before

* The biological father and biological mother meet.
* They build a small family.
* Their first son is born (Older Brother).

---

## ~24 Years Before

* The biological mother becomes pregnant again.
* Financial problems begin.

---

## ~23 Years Before

* The player is born.
* The biological mother dies during childbirth.
* The father falls into severe depression.

---

## Following Months

* Alcohol addiction begins.
* The father's life gradually collapses.
* He loses financial stability.

---

## ~22 Years Before

* The authorities determine the father cannot properly care for both children.
* The player is legally adopted.
* The older brother remains with the father.

---

## ~18 Years Before

* The older brother leaves home.
* He later joins the military.
* Contact with the family slowly disappears.

---

## Following Years

* The adoptive parents raise the player with love.
* They never reveal the adoption.
* The biological father continues living alone.
* His health slowly deteriorates.

---

# Present Day

## Day 0

* The adoptive parents pass away.
* Funeral.

---

## Day 1

* The player returns to the family house.
* Begins organizing belongings.

---

## Day 2–3

* Strange documents are discovered.
* Suspicion begins.

---

## Day 4–6

* Evidence confirms the adoption.

---

## Day 7–10

* Investigation expands.
* Hospital.
* Archive.
* Municipality.

---

## Day 11–14

* The biological father is found.

---

## Final Days

* The player spends time with him.
* The father reveals the existence of the older brother.
* Shortly afterward, he dies.

---

## Ending

* Funeral.
* Return to the old house.
* Discovery of the final family photograph.
* The player leaves carrying the photo.

Fade to black.

End of Part 1.
